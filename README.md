## Features

### Free
- **Synced lyrics** — line-by-line highlighting at sub-second precision
- **Community comments** — timed SoundCloud comments anchored below the lyric line they were written on (first 3 tracks free)
- **Click to seek** — tap any lyric line to jump to that moment
- **Fullscreen overlay** — press `F` to fill the screen with lyrics and ambient background
- **Mini panel** — compact floating panel while music plays
- **Ambient background** — colour blobs derived from album art; every track has its own mood
- **Art styles** — Default, Vinyl, CD, 3D Cube, Video
- **Artist info** — Wikipedia and Last.fm bios on demand
- **Copy active line** — `C` key in fullscreen

### Premium (one-time, lifetime)
- **Visual themes** — Glitch, Spotlight, Typewriter
- **Visualisations** — Synthwave, Reactions, Sakura, Snow
- **Unlimited community comments**

## Usage

Go to [soundcloud.com](https://soundcloud.com) and play any track. A floating button appears bottom-right — click it for the mini panel, or press `F` for fullscreen.

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| Open fullscreen | `F` or click the floating button |
| Seek to a lyric | Click any line |
| Copy active line | `C` |
| Close fullscreen | `F` or `Esc` |
| Toggle comments | Style menu → Effects |
| Change art style | Style menu → Display |
